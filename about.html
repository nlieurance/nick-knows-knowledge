About Nick

Knowledge
built to
last.

I'm a content and knowledge specialist who builds documentation systems that help people resolve problems faster. I've owned knowledge libraries across hardware, software, and AI product domains — auditing what exists, fixing what's broken, and building governance that keeps it accurate over time.

I think about content strategically: how it's structured, how it scales, how it performs inside AI search and agent-assist tools. A well-written article that's hard to find — or structured in a way AI can't parse — isn't doing its job.

I'm also experienced working cross-functionally — aligning Product, Engineering, Support, and Operations around knowledge systems, driving timely SME reviews, and making sure documentation is ready before launches.
