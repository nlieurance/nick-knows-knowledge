Knowledge Management & AI

Good knowledge is the foundation AI builds on.

AI-assisted support is only as good as the knowledge underneath it. I build and govern content systems that are structured for findability, maintained for accuracy, and optimized so AI search and recommendations surface the right answer — not the closest approximation.

I've used AI tools to accelerate drafting and auditing while keeping a quality-first approach — validating outputs, catching outdated guidance, and improving the source content that AI systems depend on.

01
Content Auditing & Remediation
Systematically identifying outdated guidance, gaps, and duplication — then executing a prioritized plan to fix it before it reaches agents or AI systems.
02
AI-Ready Knowledge Structure
Structuring articles, taxonomy, and naming conventions so AI search and recommendations surface the right content — not the closest approximation.
03
Governance & Content Lifecycle
Review cadences, approval workflows, versioning, and ownership models that keep documentation accurate, compliant, and trustworthy at scale.
04
Cross-functional Stakeholder Management
Driving timely SME reviews, aligning Product, Support, and Operations, and ensuring knowledge is ready before launches — not scrambled together after.
